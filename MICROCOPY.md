## Key Principles

1. **No marketing fluff** - Every word earns its place
2. **Action-oriented** - Tell users what to do
3. **Confident** - We know what we're doing
4. **Minimal** - Remove unnecessary words
5. **Premium** - Professional, not casual
6. **Clear** - No jargon or confusion
7. **Honest** - Realistic feedback, not false positives
